== 🥾🔵 Hike: La Tour d'Arces ⚔️🛡️ & Pierre Grosse 🪨 ==
- 🟢 Easy for good hikers
- 🔵/🔴 Medium/hard for noobs

Meet at 1pm at parking "Esplanade du Souvenir Français" near Parc Paul Mistral:
- https://www.osm.org/way/69486256
- https://goo.gl/maps/BAbHQ153PcNgunPd6

We ride our cars for about 15m. Then park our car(s) in Biviers:
- https://www.osm.org/way/892102440
- https://goo.gl/maps/xdjFtSUTK9WyxHyd9

We will hike around to the old tower "Tour d'Arces". Then see if we can find the big rock "Pierre Grosse". Go back to cars by another way and finally go back to Grenoble by cars 😎

Topo & GPX track: https://s.42l.fr/J9ML9agA
▶💡 Download GPX track on your phone. Tuto here: https://github.com/Binnette/GAC
Distance: 10km
Time: ~3/4h of hike + 30m 🚗
D+: 700m
