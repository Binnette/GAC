## ❔ What do you need ❔

- Hiking shoes 🥾
- Hiking pole (to walk in snow)
- 🧃 Water (2L minimum) + 🥕 food for lunch + 🍫 Some snack
- Clothes for wind and sun
- Sun-cream 🌞
- Your smile 😁 / Happiness 😊
- Your mask as always 😷 (avoid contact and so on)
- 💵 Money for car share

If you have any questions, please ask!
