- Hiking shoes 🥾 (or any good/non slippery shoes)
- 🧃 Water + 🥕 food for lunch + 🍫 Some snack
- Clothes for wind/rain ☔ (if any)

- Your smile 😁 / Happiness 😊
- Your mask as always 😷 (avoid contact and so on)
- 💵 Money for car share
